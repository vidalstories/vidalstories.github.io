sWitched - B. Vidal's Debut YA Paranormal Novel

Prepare for a thrilling and haunting ride through the world of *sWitched*, B. Vidal's debut young adult paranormal novel, launching on March 1, 2025!

About the Book

*sWitched* tells the story of a teenager who discovers her life is intertwined with supernatural forces beyond her understanding. As she navigates the complexities of adolescence, she must confront ancient secrets, fight dark forces, and discover what it truly means to be 'switched.' The novel combines mystery, paranormal intrigue, and a strong, relatable heroine in a gripping narrative.
