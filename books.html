Switched

Release Date: March 1, 2025

"Switched" is a genre-bending story that follows the journey of two seemingly unconnected individuals whose lives become intertwined in unexpected and life-changing ways. The book explores themes of identity, fate, and the consequences of choices.

The story unfolds with gripping tension, as each chapter reveals secrets, twists, and revelations that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. Whether you're a fan of thrillers, drama, or speculative fiction, "Switched" is sure to resonate.
